My Capstone

Solar Powered Dichromatic Lighting System

This study focuses primarily on the development of solar streetlight design that incorporates two colors of light, namely white and yellow, complemented by an integrated emergency button and SMS alert system. The aim of this study was to enhance visibility in urban areas and provide a means for immediate assistance during emergencies.
